About Lindsay and Stephen

photo: IHNY Photography

Lindsay and Stephen met in the fall of 2013, right after their beloved Cardinals and Red Sox played in the World Series. Lindsay’s team may have lost, but in the game of life, she certainly won! They quickly hit it off and have been inseparable ever since. In April 2017, Stephen popped the question during a weekend in New York City using a characteristically elaborate plan (ask him about the engagement jeans!).

A native of Southern Arizona who now loves her adopted city, Lindsay might not have lasted this long if she hadn't met and fallen in love with Boston local Stephen during her first New England winter. Thankfully they did fall in love, and they have grown closer through their shared passions for learning and making a difference.

Lindsay is honing her public speaking skills as a member of the Longwood Toastmasters and Stephen has been learning French for several years as a member of Boston’s French Cultural Center / Alliance Française. Both are learning to code, with Lindsay mastering Python for her work in fundraising data science and Stephen tackling several languages to build this website from scratch!

Lindsay is on the board of the Young Nonprofit Professionals Network of Boston, which provides networking and development opportunities for young leaders in the nonprofit sector, while for the past 5 years, Stephen has led a volunteer group that provides weekly tutoring sessions for students preparing for the high school equivalency (Hi-SET / GED) exam.

The couple also loves traveling, keeping their local Starbucks in business, and cheering on the Cards and Sox!

They are excited to welcome everyone and celebrate at their wedding!
